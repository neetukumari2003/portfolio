Research Work

Tor Snowflake Bridge: A WebRTC Traffic Analysis for Privacy Enhancement

Published in COMSNETS 2025

Conducted comprehensive network traffic analysis to explore obfuscation techniques employed by Tor Bridge Snowflake in circumventing censorship. Utilized Wireshark to analyze and compare traffic patterns with common web activities, identifying key similarities between obfuscated Snowflake traffic and WebRTC-based protocols. This research deepened insights into anonymity and anti-censorship features while distinguishing Snowflake traffic from standard web browsing.
